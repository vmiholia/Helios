We see a conflicting trend in the data:
*   **Activity is High:** She averages high step counts (~11k/day), yet...
*   **Weight is Rebounding:** +7.3 kg gained since July 2025 (Low: 65.0kg -> Current: 72.3kg).
*   **Heart Health is Declining:** Resting HR has risen from 76 bpm to **81.4 bpm**, indicating stress or lack of recovery.

### The "Chaakraa" Factor
*   **Category:** **Cardio + HIIT (High Intensity Interval Training)**.
*   **Effect:** These rhythm-based rides are excellent for caloric burn and heart health (VO2 Max). However, they are *high stress* on the body (cortisol).
*   **Adjustment:** Since she is now adding 2 intense cardio sessions/week, we must **NOT** add more high-intensity cardio. The remaining days must focus on **Recovery** and **Pure Strength** to build the muscle she lacks.

## 3. The 3-Month Blueprint

**Goal:** Leverage the Spinning classes for fat burn, but use Strength training to lower Body Age.

### Phase 1: Integration & Protection (Weeks 1-4)
*Focus: Fueling the Spinning classes and protecting muscle.*

*   **Nutrition:**
    *   **Calorie Target:** ~1,700 kcal.
    *   **Protein Non-Negotiable:** **100g - 120g daily**. She *must* eat protein after spinning to prevent muscle breakdown.
    *   **Carb Timing:** Eat carbs (fruit, oats, rice) *before/after* Spinning. Low carb on other days.
*   **Weekly Schedule:**
    *   **Spinning (2 Days):** Go hard. This is the "Engine" work.
    *   **Strength (2 Days):** **Slow & Heavy.**
        *   *Focus:* Squats, Lunges, Push-ups, Overhead Press.
        *   *Why?* Spinning ignores the upper body and doesn't build bone density like heavy weights do.
    *   **Steps (Daily):** Cap at **8k - 10k**. Do not push for more. Recovery is now more important.

### Phase 2: Metabolic Shift (Weeks 5-8)
*Focus: Accelerating Fat Loss.*

*   **Nutrition:**
    *   Introduce **Intermittent Fasting (14:10)** on *non-spinning* days only.
*   **Training Adjustment:**
    *   The 2 Strength days must become "Progressive" (add weight or reps).
    *   If energetic, add 1 "Active Recovery" Yoga/Pilates session to lower that RHR.

### Phase 3: Peak Performance (Weeks 9-12)
*Focus: Driving the Body Age down.*

*   **Metric Check:** If RHR hasn't dropped to <77 bpm, we reduce spinning intensity and increase sleep.
*   **Push:** Increase weights in the gym. The more muscle she has, the faster the fat burns off during Spinning.
